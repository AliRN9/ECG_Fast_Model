# ECG_Fast_Model

Sirius University,  
Department of Information Technology and Artificial Intelligence,  
Mathematical modelling in biomedicine and petreleum engineering.

Fundamental model:  
Monodomain + Full ECG  

Optimization:  
1) Discrete method   
2) Lead field method
